Frequently Asked Questions

Is Tendy really free?
Yes, 100% free. All matching, alerts, and dashboard features are included. We offer optional paid consulting services, but the core tool is free.
How do you know which contracts match my business?
We use your NAICS codes, set-aside eligibility, and location to match you with qualified opportunities. You tell us what you bid on, and we learn your preferences over time.
Can I change my preferences later?
Yes. You can update your NAICS codes, geographic regions, opportunity values, and other preferences anytime from your dashboard.
How often are opportunities updated?
Real-time. We check SAM.gov every 30 minutes for new postings. You'll get alerts as soon as matches are published.
Do you share my data?
Never. Your profile, preferences, and tracked opportunities are private. We don't sell or share any data.
What if I'm already using SAM.gov directly?
